Common Inquiries

Questions & Answers

How do I initiate a pre-order?

Simply reach out via Instagram DM or Email with the item details. Our concierge team will guide you through the reservation and procurement stages.

What is the Minimum Order Quantity?

We accommodate every scale. From a singular statement piece for your home to bulk industrial shipments, our service remains uncompromising.

Can you source items not listed?

Exclusivity is our specialty. Provide us with an image or specification of your desired goods, and our global sourcing network will locate the finest options available.

When are orders typically closed?

Order windows are strategically managed to ensure consolidation and timely shipping batches. Contact us for the current cycle's closing dates.

What is the transit duration to Kenya?

Air Freight typically arrives within 15 days, while Sea Freight follows a 35 to 45-day window, depending on consolidation schedules.

Is freight settled before or after arrival?

Shipping fees are generally settled upon the arrival of your goods in Kenya, providing you with peace of mind throughout the transit process.
